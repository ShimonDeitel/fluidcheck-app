Privacy Policy

Fluid Levels Checklist is an offline-first app. All data you enter (such as fluid name, level status, and checked date) is stored only in the app's local storage on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data, and the app makes no network calls to any server we operate.

Subscription purchases are processed entirely by Apple's StoreKit framework; we never see your payment details.
